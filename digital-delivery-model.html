Purpose

Design and implement the enterprise operating model that transforms Mars Snacking from project-based delivery into a product and platform organization capable of delivering continuous business value.

What we do

Design the future operating model

Create the blueprint for how Digital Technologies should operate, including product and platform structures, value streams, governance, decision rights, funding, and organizational design.

Lead enterprise transformation

Drive the change from projects to products: temporary funding to persistent product investment, functional silos to cross-functional product teams, delivery outputs to business outcomes.

Embed new ways of working

Coach leaders, align business and technology, define governance, introduce common metrics, and scale Agile and DevOps to drive adoption across the organization.

How we can help

  • Design the product and platform operating model.
  • Prioritize investment through a value-based portfolio framework.
  • Standardize governance and ways of working.
  • Enable the shift from projects to products.
  • Measure business value through outcomes and KPIs.
